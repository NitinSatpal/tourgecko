Pricing

5% is the platform (tourgecko) fee. The integrated payment gateway fee is 2% + 3 Rs. But the good thing is you can transfer the integrated payment gateway fee to your buyer by switiching ON the 'convenience fee' in your payment settings.

Let's suppose you have a tour of 1000 Rs. When you get a booking. The following distribution will take place:

  • Considering integrated payment gateway fee (convenience fee) is transferred to the buyer

    • 5% of 1000 = 50 Rs. will be transferred to platform (tourgecko)

    • You get 950 Rs. in your bank account

  • Considering you are not transferring integrated payment gateway fee (convenience fee) to the buyer

    • 5% of 1000 = 50 Rs. will be transferred to platform (tourgecko)

    • 2% of 1000 = 20 + 3 i.e 23 Rs. will be transferred to payment gateway

    • 18% GST on 23 = 4.14 Rs. will be levied as taxes

    • You get 922.60 Rs. in your bank account
